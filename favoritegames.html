The Battle System of…

What is this?

This is a series of write-ups I made about some of my favorite turn-based battle systems, compiled and written during my Fall 2024 semester at USC.

In other words, this is a collection of write-ups on cool JRPG battle systems.

Turn-based JRPGs are my favorite games to play, and these are some systems that stand out in my mind as exemplary and worth chronicling.
I hope to use these analyses when making games of my own, but also set out making them with the goal of sharing them with others, like you!
All pixel art here is mine. Enjoy!
