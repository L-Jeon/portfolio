Daigon Express Railway

Released October 2022

Guide the trains to their destinations!
Winner of Bear Jams Fall 2022.

A simple puzzle game, made for UC Berkeley's Bear Jams Fall 2022,
a 45-hour game jam held from October 28th to October 30th.
Estimated playtime of 5-10 minutes.

Credits: Art, UI. Was also project lead.

Interwoven

Scheduled for Winter 2022 - Demo Available

Follow an unlikely trio on their journey up to defeat the Obsidian Drake atop Anvite Mountain. Features an unique Augment Battle System, which you can utilize to create all sorts of interesting strategies.

A turn-based RPG with visual novel-style story cutscenes. The demo contains about an hour of content.

Credits: Producer, Designer, Artist

Steam Page (includes link to demo)

Ghost Quest:
The Phantom Game

Released May 2020

You play as a ghost, who was kicked out of your body by an evil force. Left with the power to travel between the material and spiritual world, you embark on a journey to take your body back from the rude being that robbed you.

A bite-sized JRPG experience (~5-10 minutes).

Credits: Producer, Director, Artist, Lead Programmer, etc.
